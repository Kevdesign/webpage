mHand H1-B

Designed and developed for Megabyte Limited by Kevin Szeto while working in Stratos Design.

mHand H1-B is a mobile device for stocktaking with various antenna options and ease of connectivity to Internet of Things. Its slim, ergonomic design with adjustable reading power is suitable for retail operation. The near-field antenna is designed for short range proximity reading while the far-field circular or linear antenna is designed for long-range reading. Its ‘Plug and Go’ mechanics makes easy antenna switching. The reader can connect with any Bluetooth enabled devices, OS independent, providing a seamless connectivity with all mobile devices including smartphone, tablet, notebook and laptop. This helps increase the UHF RFID mobility and connectivity over a network.

Awards:
▪ Hong Kong ICT Awards 2014 : Best Innovation (Innovative Technology) Award : Bronze Award
▪ Hong Kong RFID Award 2013 : Most Innovative EPC / RFID Products : Gold Award

MegaTray

Designed and developed for Megabyte Limited by Kevin Szeto while working in Stratos Design.

MegaTray, essentially a jewelry display tray with a discreetly integrated RFID reader and Wi-Fi transceiver to provide security and monitoring capabilities for items placed on it. Each item displayed on the tray is given a unique RFID tag, allowing the Megatray device to conduct an automated item count during the sales process to prevent employee theft and shoplifting. 
A lot of time was invested into designing the device with high user acceptance among sales staff working in jewelry and watch shops. The device was designed to mimic a traditional display tray in terms of weight and appearance, and is portable.

Awards:
▪ Hong Kong ICT Awards 2011 - Best Ubiquitous Networking (Mobile Enterprise Solution) Award : Gold Award
▪  Hong Kong RFID Awards 2010 - Most Innovative EPC / RFID Products: Gold Award
