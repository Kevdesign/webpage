About KEVIN SZETO

Kevin is a multi-talented Industrial Designer,
he received formal training in Product Design, Product Engineering and Marketing.
With his competency in both creative design skills and advanced technical knowledge,
he helped his clients to develop innovative products
by injecting creative insight, stylish design and effective engineering solutions.
