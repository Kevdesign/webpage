About KEVIN SZETO


Kevin is a multi-talented Industrial Designer with more than 15 years product development experience.
He received formal training in Product Design, Product Engineering and Marketing.
With his competency in both creative design skills and advanced technical knowledge, he helped his clients to develop innovative products by injecting creative insight, stylish design and effective engineering solutions.
