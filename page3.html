FlexiWhisk

Created by Daka International Ltd. in-house design team while Kevin Szeto was the Senior Designer in the team.

FlexiWhisk allows users to change the volume of the whisk according to different purposes. They just need to press a soft rubber button in order to adjust the size of the whisk and meet different requirements. For example, the smallest shape is suitable for the use in small bowls, while the next size is recommended for beating egg whites. The third size is used for mixing batter while the largest size makes stirring sauces easy et cetera. 
The FlexiWhisk owes its high ease of use to the conclusive realisation of an innovative design.

Award:
Red Dot Design Award Winner 2016

Tongner

Designed by Daka International Ltd. in-house design team while Kevin Szeto was the Senior Designer in the team.

Tongner easily transforms from a spatula into a pair of tongs and vice versa, thanks to its integrated magnets. Two sets of magnets, on heat-resistant pair inside the head of the spatula and a second pair inside the handle, allow users to quickly convert the tool by simply moving its two components to the right position. The handle of this space-saving kitchen utensil is made of soft rubber, which lends it a pleasant feel. 
Tongner convinces as an ergonomically sophisticated and user-friendly product solution of high formal quality.

Award:
Red Dot Design Award Winner 2016

Scolander

Designed by Daka International Ltd. in-house design team while Kevin Szeto was the Senior Designer in the team.

Scolander is a multi-functional Scoop and Colander in one tool. With the easy slide button mechanism, users can pull the button to strain at anytime. It is easy to scoop food with the thin edge and flat surface design. Quick and efficient scoop and drain food straight from a pan or oven dish.
